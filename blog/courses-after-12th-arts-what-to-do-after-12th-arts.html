As the 12th board exams approach their conclusion, a sense of anticipation and excitement takes over for many students. However, these feelings can also be mingled with confusion and uncertainty, particularly for Arts students who might be inundated with the question: "What to do after 12th Arts?"

Indeed, the academic pathway after 12th Arts is as diverse as it is promising, offering opportunities that pave the way for fascinating career prospects. This blog aims to bring clarity to these pathways and help students explore some of the most popular courses after 12th Arts.

How to Choose the Right Course After 12th Arts?

Choosing what to pursue after 12th Arts requires careful consideration of certain factors, including interests, aptitude, career goals, and job market trends. Here are a few steps to help students make the right choice:

  • The first step in the process is to identify the areas of interest in school years and decide which profession best suits the student's long-term career goals.
  • In searching for the right program after 12th arts, students should not limit their research to just the internet or books. They should also take the help of study abroad consultants to get detailed information on all the related course options and complete profile assessment.
  • Students should look at the course duration, as this can vary from one course to another, along with the total cost involved in pursuing the course abroad, including living expenses.
  • Students should also evaluate whether the chosen field matches their skills and strengths. For instance, a course in fine arts would require creative skills, whereas a course in journalism would require good communication skills.
  • Considering the trends in the job market is another factor to keep in mind, as some fields may have more job opportunities than others
  • Once all the relevant information is available, shortlist and compare a few courses on various factors like course content, job prospects, cost, duration, etc., before finally applying for one.

FAQ

What are some common courses available for students after completing 12th grade in the Arts stream?
There is a wide range of course options available to students after 12th grade. Some of the popular courses include Bachelor's in Fine Arts (BFA); Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM); Bachelor's in Arts (BA) in various disciplines such as English, Psychology, Sociology, History, and Political Science; Bachelor's in Design (B.Des); Bachelor's in Business Administration (BBA); Bachelor's in Fashion Designing; Bachelor of Social Work (BSW); and Bachelor's in Mass Communication, among others.
Can I switch to a Science or Commerce related course after 12th grade in Arts?
Some universities and institutions may allow students for such a switch, but it is generally rare. While science courses require a background in science from high school, commerce or business-related courses may be more flexible. Therefore, it is suggested to check the specific requirements for the course at the institution before applying for it.
What career prospects are available to students post the completion of their degree course after 12th Arts?
The choice of career any student opts to pursue is based on several factors. However, Arts students can go on to have successful careers in various fields, such as teaching, graphic design, journalism, social work, fashion design, interior design, business, advertising, and public relations.

While some colleges and universities admit students based on their 12th-grade marks, others require students to pass an entrance exam. Can I pursue professional courses like law and hotel management after 12th Arts?
Yes, students from the Arts field can pursue professional courses like law and hotel management. Admission to these courses is generally through entrance exams in major institutions, hence it is suggested to always check the admission guidelines before applying for one.
