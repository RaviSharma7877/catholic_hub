The United Kingdom is globally known for its top-ranked universities and high standard of education, making it a popular destination for international students. One of the trending courses among graduates today is the Masters in Business Analytics, which equips students with the skills and knowledge to leverage data-driven decision-making in the business world.
 
This blog provides details on top universities offering this program in UK, including eligibility criteria, scholarships, and other requirements.

Why Study Masters in Business Analytics?

Studying a Masters in Business Analytics in UK can be a strategic choice for several reasons:

  • UK has some of the globally recognised business schools
  • The MS in Business Analytics in UK is a comprehensive and multidisciplinary program, combining aspects of business strategy, data science, and information management
  • The program provides the knowledge and skills that students need to manage, analyse, and interpret complex data in a business context
  • Universities in UK often has strong ties with the industry and provides students with opportunities for internships, project work, and networking. Such experiences enhance the practical experience of students and increase their employability after graduation
  • Studying in the multicultural environment of UK offers the opportunity to build a global network of professional contacts
  • Post-study work visa offered by UK government helps students gain international work experience upon completion of their studies

Masters (MS) in Business Analytics in UK: Eligibility

The eligibility for MS in Business Analytics in UK varies from university to university, but students can make sure they fulfil these common requirements:

  • A bachelor's or equivalent degree from a recognised university. Few universities require a degree in a quantitative discipline such as mathematics, statistics, economics, engineering, or computer science
  • Evidence of English language proficiency through tests like IELTS or TOEFL score
  • Letters of recommendation (LOR) and a personal statement or essay
  • GMAT or GRE score (if required)
  • Work experience (if needed)

Masters (MS) in Business Analytics in UK: Application Requirements

The application process typically involves the following:

  • A complete online application form
  • Submission of transcripts from previous university studies
  • A statement of purpose outlining the motivation to study Business Analytics, career goals, and interest in the chosen university
  • Letters of recommendation from professors or employers
  • Test scores, including English language proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL) and possibly GRE or GMAT scores
  • A CV/resume may be required, especially if the program requires or prefers work experience
  • An interview may be part of the process, often conducted online for international students

Masters (MS) in Business Analytics in UK: Scholarships

Many universities offer scholarships for international students based on their academic merit, financial need, or both. Some Examples include:

Chevening Scholarships: These scholarships are awarded to outstanding scholars from around the world planning to study for a masters degree in any subject at any UK university.

Commonwealth Scholarships: As the name suggests, these scholarships are offered to students from developing Commonwealth countries pursuing Masters, PhD, and split-site (PhD) study.

University-specific scholarships: Many universities also offer their own scholarships to international students. Students check with their study abroad consultants to find more information.

Conclusion

A Masters in Business Analytics from a top university in UK can significantly enhance students' career prospects in today's data-driven business world. By understanding the eligibility criteria, scholarship opportunities, and application requirements, students can take the first steps toward a successful academic and professional journey in the field of business analytics.

FAQ

Can a student pursue MSc in Business Analytics in UK without work experience?
Yes. Students demonstrating excellent academic merit and coursework in the quantitative field can seek admission to the program without any professional experience.
Is MS in Business Analytics a STEM degree in UK?
Yes, Business Analytics is a STEM-designated program, providing two years of optional practical training (OPT) for international students.
Is a GMAT score required to apply for Masters in Business Analytics in UK?
Most universities in UK require GMAT scores for admission into a Masters in Business Analytics course. It is always better to inquire with experts at Canam to know the details of the chosen course.
What is the minimum percentage requirement for masters in UK?
The percentage requirement for masters in UK varies from institute to institute. However, the generally acceptable percentage ranges between 65%-80% in graduation.
Is Business Analytics a demanding field in UK?
MSc in business analytics is a popular course for postgraduate students worldwide. It opens a plethora of opportunities for students in the business sector post the completion of their degree.
