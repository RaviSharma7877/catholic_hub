New Zealand is gaining popularity as an excellent study-abroad destination among students, offering world-class education using innovative technology-driven teaching methods at affordable costs. The country hosts universities with top QS World Rankings, providing a wide range of program options. Universities in New Zealand accept admission for two significant intakes, January and July.

Read further to know in detail the timeline for intakes, universities and the courses offered in New Zealand.

Steps to Study in New Zealand for July 2023 Intake

July, also known as Spring intake, is a popular intake among international students thinking to pursue their education in New Zealand. Students can choose from diverse program options, such as undergraduate, postgraduate and short-term courses. Following are the steps to apply for July 2023 intake:
Step 1: Begin with exploring the programs that best match the profile and shortlist the universities offering preferred courses.
Step 2: Prepare and attempt for necessary test, such as GMAT, IELTS, PTE, etc., for admission.
Step 3: Apply to the selected university/ college before application deadline.
Step 4: Draft personal essay known as Statement of Purpose (SOP) and provide Letter of Recommendation (LOR) if required by the admission authorities of the chosen academic institute.
Step 5: Keep a check on the emails for acceptance letter issued by the admission department and timely revert to the mail.
Step 6: Prepare for interview.
Step 7: Make application for student visa and arrange funds required for study abroad.
Step 8: Book flight tickets and fly before session begins.

Steps to Study in New Zealand for January 2024 Intake

The January intake in the universities of New Zealand commences in February, and the semester ends in the month of November. It is best to start the application process six months before the intake begins. Here is step-by-step process to apply for January 2024 intake:
Step 1: Search and shortlist the courses available for the intake.
Step 2: Prepare for necessary standardized test required for admission.
Step 3: Begin the application process by applying to the selected university/ college.
Step 4: Prepare Statement of Purpose (SOP) and Letter of Recommendation (LOR) as these are essential documents required by the admission authorities of the chosen academic institute.
Step 5: Accept the offer letter sent by the admission department and prepare for interview.
Step 6: Apply for student visa and education loan.
Step 7: Arrange accommodation, book flight tickets and prepare for the session.

FAQ

How many intakes are available in New Zealand?

New Zealand offers two major intakes in a year- January and the July intake.

Which is the most popular intake in New Zealand?

The most popular intake is the July or Spring intake in New Zealand. It begins in July and ends in November. This intake offers a wide range of programs for students.

Which English proficiency test is accepted in New Zealand?

English Proficiency Tests such as IELTS, TOEFL and PTE are accepted in New Zealand.

How much gap is acceptable to study in New Zealand?

Even a 10 years gap is accepted in New Zealand on the basis of relevant work experience, studies and skills development in that time.

What is the IELTS Band Score requirement for a study visa in New Zealand?

A minimum overall IELTS Band Score of 6 is required to apply for study in New Zealand.
