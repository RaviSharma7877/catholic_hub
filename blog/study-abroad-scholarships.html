Study Abroad Scholarships

Do you think, you're eligible for an international scholarship? If so, you should be aware that scholarships for studying abroad help students pay for their college tuition by offering financial support. Scholarships are awarded on the basis of academic or athletic achievements. Additionally, study abroad scholarships for Indian students have opened the possibility of studying abroad to a broader range of students.

According to a recent research, more than 10 lakh Indian students study abroad in more than 85 nations. In other words, scholarships enable Indian students to attend prestigious universities without worrying about paying for specific costs. 

Benefits of Study abroad scholarships

Studying abroad allows you to apply to diverse courses and universities. Once you graduate, you can begin to pursue your career with decent salary. Additionally, employers often choose students with overseas education due to their quality of education and exposure. Other than that, the benefits of studying abroad include opportunities for employment and recognition worldwide. Moreover, you get a:

1.Quality education in good universities.

2.Access to prestigious courses without financial pressure.

3.Moreover, getting a scholarship encourages improving your co-curricular and academic performance.

4.A merit-based scholarship lets the university know about your capabilities and your ability to cope with its curriculum. Hence, contributing to their university.

5.Opting for a fully-funded scholarship helps you bear the 5% increase in international tuition fees. 

Five Popular Undergraduate Scholarships for Indian Students

Study Abroad Scholarships often become an asset in your CVs, proving extremely beneficial. Nevertheless, living in another country involves various other expenses, such as living, transport, personal fees, etc. Hence, having a scholarship for studying abroad is genuinely beneficial.

In today's pool of scholarships for international students, here are the top 5 scholarships that Indian students should aim for: 

Oxford and Cambridge Society of India (OCSI) Scholarships

If you want to study abroad, apply for the OCSI scholarship, a Delhi-based nonprofit organization. Moreover, Indian students who wish to consider the Universities of Cambridge or Oxford may do so through OCSI. They provide benefits of INR 2, 00,000.

The eligibility criterion for OCSI scholarship is as follows: 

1.You can enroll in a part-time or full-time program at Oxford, Cambridge, or any of their affiliated colleges.

2.You are an Indian citizen.

3.If you are 30 years as on 1 September of the year of application. 

QS Undergraduate Scholarship

Quacquarelli Symonds offers a scholarship for a bachelor's degree. QS Scholarship is given to candidates on a merit basis to pursue a course at any QS-ranked institution.The maximum benefit you receive is INR 3, 68,000 to cover the tuition fees, with the following eligibility criteria:

1.A QS-ranking accredited institution must accept the applying candidate, and candidates must begin their first academic year immediately after completing their last education.

UCD Global Graduate Scholarships 

UCD Global Graduate Scholarships are among the most renowned international scholarships for Indian students studying in Dublin. Mainly, this scholarship is for students who wish to learn at one of UCD's institutions. Further, UCD Scholarships are merit-based, providing awards with either 50% or 100 % of tuition fees.

The eligibility criteria are as follows:

1.Provide proof of acceptance into a UCD program.

2.You must be a non-EU student and should have enrolled in self-funding for tuition fees.

3.Make sure that you are a permanent citizen of any one of the eligible countries.

Wien International Scholarship Program

Wien International Scholarship Program has sent over 894 scholars to study abroad at Brandeis University. Further, in 1958 this study abroad scholarship program started. A need-based scholarship that covers the entire tuition fee is available to those who meet the following requirements:

1.Get accepted to Brandeis University

2.You must be eligible for financial assistance.

3.The candidate must have the required academic score.

Cornell University TATA Scholarship

Tata Scholarship is the best scholarship for Indian students to study abroad for undergrad courses, and Tata Education and Development Trust fund this course. The annual scholarship helps Indian students to pursue their careers at Cornell University. However, Tata Scholarship is available to only 20 students and is a need-based scholarship, covering the entire tuition fee.

Here is the eligibility criterion:

1.You must be an Indian citizen.

2.Have you completed secondary schooling in India?

3.If you are eligible for need-based financial help.

4.You must have received an offer letter from Cornell University.

Studying abroad scholarships can work toward a brighter future. Besides, scholarships are a great way to turn your study abroad dreams into reality. Therefore, you should never hesitate to apply for such golden opportunities if you have merit or are in need. Other than that, when you plan to study abroad, you have to prepare for the IELTS exam and appear for it to achieve high band scores in it.

FAQ

What are the requirements to get study abroad scholarships?
To seek study abroad scholarships, you need to look at the eligibility criteria for each of these scholarships. Therefore, each of these scholarships’ criteria needs to be considered before applying.
How much percentage is required to apply for scholarships in foreign countries?
A student needs to have a percentage between 60 to 85% in their class 12th Board examinations to be eligible to apply for scholarships abroad. 
Is it hard to get scholarships to study abroad?
Finding a scholarship to study abroad can be difficult. So, you should look at official university websites or government websites that give information about these scholarships. 
What exams do I need to sit for to seek study abroad scholarships?
You need to give some of the following exams to get study abroad scholarships:

•IELTS
•GRE
•GMAT
•TOEFL
•SAT
•PTE

Which country offers the easiest scholarships?
China is the easiest country to apply for scholarships.
