In-Person

Highlight

Representative visits are one of a kind event where representatives from partner and potential partner universities and colleges from across the world visit Canam offices. The meets are organized to strengthen organizational relationships by discussing application and selection processes and pain points.

  • Student contexts
  • Application and Selection Processes
  • Student Psychology
  • Services before Admission & to International Students

Partnerships with institutes work two ways. These events are an attempt for both organizations to strengthen bonds as business partners, education facilitators and providers where both sides need to understand and improve the processes to deliver better.

The event helps Canam understand the role an institute plays in the student's life in a foreign land and presents its role as a facilitator in the admission process. Canam cherishes these visits for they help in keeping communication transparent and efficient and provide the best of services to its clients.
