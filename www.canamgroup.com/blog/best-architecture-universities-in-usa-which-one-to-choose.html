Architecture is the field of science and art wherein the architects design buildings and other physical structures on ground. This field will help you exhibit your creativity while studying scientific subjects of shapes and dimensions. Therefore, if you pursue a degree in architecture then it will provide you an opportunity to practice your brilliance and creativity in science of designing buildings. Are you wondering where to pursue the course? Especially, which university in the USA? Choosing, therefore, the best architecture university in USA will help you live your dreams of making a career in this field of creativity, science and brilliance.

What is the eligibility to study architecture in the USA?

Students who wish to study architecture in a university or college in USA have to fulfill certain eligibility requirements. These requirements vary for various universities or colleges. The eligibility criteria for studying architecture in university in USA is as follows:

•A Bachelor degree in architecture or a related field
•GMAT/GRE Scores
•English language proficiency tests like IELTS or TOEFL scores
•Statement of Purpose (SOP)
•CV/Resume
•Letters of Recommendation (LORs)

What are the benefits of studying architecture in USA?

There are various benefits of studying architecture in USA. These are as follows:

  • USA has best universities in the world that offer architecture programs at various levels of degree like Masters, Bachelors or Associate level.
  • A five-year B.Arch and a M.Arch are the only two degrees that are recognized by National Council of Architectural Registration Boards (NCARB) in theUSA where the board grants license of Architect to these degree holders.
  • Annual salaries of architects are expected to rise quite substantially over next few years.
  • Jobs in architecture are also expected to grow in double digits over the same year.

At what levels can I study architectural programs in USA?

A student can study architectural programs in USA at associate, bachelor’s or master’s levels. These degrees are accredited by National Architectural Accrediting Board (NAAB) in USA. The programs at Bachelor level are typically five to three years long. The programs, on the other hand, at master level are two to three years in duration. They span around four to six semesters.

Conclusion
Pursuing an architecture course in USA, therefore, develops skills that will help a student show their brilliance in science and mathematics as well as creative juices. The salaries offered for various job roles after studying architecture in USA are also great with bright prospects.
