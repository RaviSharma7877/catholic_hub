Eligibility to study interior design in the UK universities

The eligibility criteria to seek admission in interior designing courses in UK universities will vary depending upon the level of program you are applying to and the university. However, below is a general list of admission requirements:

  • Class 12 with high academic standards for bachelor’s program
  • A bachelor’s degree with high academic standards for master’s program
  • Academic Transcripts of all qualifications
  • An English language proficiency test scores like IELTS
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Letters of Recommendation
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)/Resume

Costs incurred to study interior design in UK

Apart from the tuition costs that are to be incurred on the interior design course, UK is expensive to live. So, you will have to consider the living expenses as well. UK cities being mostly metropolitan in nature, living expenses here can be high.
 
Though these costs of studying in UK can be pretty high but you can think of applying for scholarships to study the course in UK. Other than the scholarships, your career and job prospects after doing the course can be quite bright. Therefore, let’s first look at the scholarships you can apply for.

Job prospects after studying interior design in the UK

Interior designers with a masters or bachelors in interior design have ample job opportunities available in UK. United Kingdom being a developed and growing economy offers great career prospects to interior designers. After graduating, you can look for jobs in the following fields with average salaries.

Jobs
Interior designer
Kitchen designer
Landscaper
Architectural designer
Design manager

Studying an interior design course in UK, therefore, can open up several job openings for you. Hence, pursuing the course will definitely brighten your future prospects.

FAQ

Which UK institute offers the best interior design course?

The following institutes in UK are considered to be the best for pursuing an interior design course as after completing the course from here, there will be ample job opportunities available:
•Royal College of Art, London
•University of Arts, London
•Glasgow School of Arts

What is the cost of studying a BA (Hons.) in interior design in UK?
A BA (Hons.) in interior design in UK will cost around 10,000 to 20,000 pounds to an international student.
Is it easy to get a job after completing an interior design course in UK?
After completing an interior design course in UK, a student will have ample job opportunities available. They can work in following job roles:
•    Interior designer
•    Kitchen Designer
•    Landscaper
•    Architectural Designer
•    Design Manager
They can earn on average 60,000 pounds in a year.
What is the most popular Bachelor level course in the UK?
The Bachelor in Arts (Hons.) is the most popular bachelor level program in interior design in the UK.
What professions are there for students to pursue after completing an interior design course in the UK?
The graduates in interior design in UK can work in various job roles. These are as follows:
•    Architects
•    Architectural technologists
•    Estates managers
•    Furniture designers
•    Graphic designers
