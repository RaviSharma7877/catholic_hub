Canadian institutions offer a unique combination of quality education at affordable tuition fees. Canada's education system is emerging amongst the leading worldwide, the number of international students flying to study in Canada increases every year.

Different offerings of both on and off-campus study facilities, the flexible and easy application process, lively campus atmosphere and skill-based courses, amongst others, make Canada a perfect study abroad destination. If you are interested in a short-term course in Canada, this blog will help you acquaint yourself with some of the most liked and preferred 1-year courses available in Canada.

An overview of 1 Year Courses in Canada

For students, including International Students who wish to study abroad and pursue a short-term course in Canada, say, 1-year period. 1-year courses in Canada offer a wide range of disciplines, renowned ones among includes:

  • Management
  • Customer Service
  • Disaster and Emergency Management
  • Accounting
  • Human Resources
  • Information Systems Security

The courses include certificate, diploma, advanced diploma and graduate diploma extending to selective postgraduate programs, like 1-year MBA or an Executive MBA. Such programs may or may not include a research area as part of the program. Precisely, the 1-year MBA or Executive MBA in Canada is a stand-out program as it is generally chosen by the ones looking forward to pursuing short-term postgraduate courses.

Admission Requirements for 1 Year course in Canada

The process of applying for 1-year courses in Canada is like that of a Bachelors or PGDM in Canada.

  • For a 1-year PG diploma course in Canada, the conditions may differ between possessing (10+2) to an undergraduate, which depends on the subject, for instance, Engineering or Business Management, and the university.ï¿½
  • For a 1-year masters program, universities need students to hold a high ranking undergraduate degree from an accredited institution.
  • Some Canadian universities and colleges may add some additional criteria to the admission process. The basic application package includes a Statement of Purpose (SOP), Letters of Recommendation (LORs), GRE/GMAT scores (optional) and English language tests such as TOEFL/IELTS.

1-year PG diploma courses in Canada are offered at low tuition fees. Moreover, the scholarship schemes and financial support opportunities provide added incentives for international students.

If you want to study in Canada, Canam's expert counsellors can guide you throughout the application process and make sure that you take the right step towards a rewarding career.
