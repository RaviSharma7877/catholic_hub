Masters (MS) Software Engineering in USA

An MS in Software Engineering in the USA will help a student develop complex software systems in a team environment. The studies and subjects in MS in Software Engineering are typically focused on the technical and organizational methods, practices and tools that are vital to developing these software systems. An MS in Software Engineering is different from MS in Computer Science because computer sciences teach a student the foundations of computing whereas software engineering teaches her to solve real-world’s complex problems with the help of engineering practices. A degree in software engineering spans subjects across software requirements, interactive designs, architecture etc.

FAQ

Are Software Engineers in demand in the USA?

The demand for Software Engineers in the USA is ever rising. As per the U.S. Bureau of Labour Statistics, the demand for software engineers in the USA is going to increase by 11% by 2029.

Is the degree of Masters useful in the field of Software Engineering?

Though one doesn’t need a Master degree to become a software engineer, but it can help the student to gain advanced knowledge of the subject and get a higher salary package.

What is the duration for a MS in Software Engineering in the USA?

The MS in Software Engineering in the USA is generally for a duration of 12 to 24 months.

What salary is offered to a MS in Software Engineering graduate in the USA?

The average annual salary of a software engineer in the USA is about 90,000 USD which is approximately 72 to 80 lakh INR.

Which are the best universities to study MS in Software Engineering in the USA?

Harvard, University of Washington and Boston University are some of the top most universities to study the mentioned course in the USA.
