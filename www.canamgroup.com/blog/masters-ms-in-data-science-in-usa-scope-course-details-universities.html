Students from all across the world come to study in the USA to experience the unparalleled education offered by the universities there. One of the most popular courses to study in the USA is Masters (MS) in Data Science. It is a two years program offered by most of the universities as an MS degree. In total, 69 universities in the USA offer MS in Data Science.

To study Master's (MS) in Data Science, students are required to have studied bachelor's degree in Computer science, mathematics, economics, and Statics.

What is a Master's (MS) in Data Science in the USA?

A Master of Science in Data Science is an interdisciplinary degree program that gives you expertise in statistical modeling, data management, machine learning, data visualization, software engineering, data ethics, and user experience to meet the needs of the industry. If you want to be a leader in the field, Masters (MS) in Data Science is the degree essential for you. It allows students to go deep into how to study and harness accumulated data, including large, complex sets known as "Big Data."

Why Study MS in Data Science in the USA?

The demand for Data scientists is always growing. Organizations are looking for candidates with the skills to collect, manage and analyze data, its types, and trends and deliver the results. When it comes to the US, the demand for data scientists is very high.
•The job of a Data Scientist is one of the most preferred jobs in the USA, and the employee satisfaction rate for working as Data scientists are also high.
•Candidates with a Masters in Data Science in the USA are highly paid in San Francisco.
•As per reports, more than 60000 jobs will be created for data scientists in the US in the coming year.
•The country has the maximum Fortune 500 companies, the US is one of the largest and most innovative markets for Data Science and related technologies like artificial intelligence, machine learning and deep learning.

MS in Data Science as per USA requirements

The Universities in the USA are known for quality of education. To get admission to a Masters in a data science course, students must prove their eligibility and fulfill the admission requirements. The admission requirements to study Masters in Data Science in the US differs for different Universities. To get admission in your preferred University, students must meet all the requirements.

•To take admission in Data Science, students are required to submit their GRE and GMAT scores. GRE score allows American Universities to understand the academic proficiency of students. The higher the GRE score, the better the chances of admission to a top University. The minimum GRE score for MS in data science in the USA is 280, and to get admission to top universities, you must score above 310.

•Students are required to pass their higher secondary education with subjects like Physics, Chemistry and Maths.

•Students must have four years graduation degree from a STEM background. A BTech or BE degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Computing, Statistics, Physical Science, or Engineering is necessary.

•A student should have a great academic record with a good GPA in a bachelor's degree. A minimum GPA of 3 out of 4 is required for international students in graduation, and it is even higher to take admission in top institutes.

•Students are required to prove their English language proficiency if they belong to non-native english speaking countries. Universities widely accept IELTS and TOEFL scores, and the higher the scores in these examinations, the easy it will be for you to get admission to a top university.

•It is advised that students should have all the documents in proper order. The documents required include academic transcripts, evidence of financial resources, two letters of recommendation, a statement of purpose, and essays should be submitted. The students must also submit GRE, GMAT, TOEFL, and IELTS scores to every University they apply to.

•Another thing that data science universities in the USA ask for is prior work experience. It allows students with a low GRE score to get admission to universities in USA and study data science courses.

•A minimum of 2 to 3 letters of recommendation from respective faculty members or ex-employers must be submitted to the Universities.

FAQ

What is the eligibility for MS in Data Science in the USA?

To study MS in Data Science in the USA, students must have a bachelor's degree in statistics and mathematics. Students must also prove their English language proficiency by taking tests such as IELTS or TOEFL and submitting the GRE scores.

Is MS in Data Science worth it in the USA?

If you want to enhance your ability to work with parse data, MS in data science is the perfect course. It gives you unparalleled opportunities to get high paying jobs and build the career of your dreams.

Is MS in data science in the USA easy or difficult?

Data science is a difficult field, but if you pay attention, do the required hard work, and have a broad set of skills and knowledge, you can achieve unparalleled success. The main elements of data science are math, statistics, and computer science.

Is data science in demand in the USA?

MS in data science is in very high demand in the USA, and the jobs like information security analyst, software developer, data scientist, and statistician are ranked among the top 10 out of 100 jobs.

Is it better to do data science in India or abroad?

There are a lot of opportunities for Data Scientists in countries like the US and the UK. USA is a country where Data Scientists are highly paid. The US has some of the top Universities where you can study Data science and expect quality education.
