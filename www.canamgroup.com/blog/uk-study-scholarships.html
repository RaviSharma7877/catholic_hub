UK Study Scholarships

A major consideration in studying abroad is the expenses involved. What's a better option than study scholarships? There are many scholarships and grants available for Indian students who want to study in the UK to ease their burden, and unlike loans, you don’t have to repay these expenses. Every year scholarships worth more than 150 million pounds make it easier for students to study in the UK. Even though these funds don’t have to be returned, we found that less than one-third of students apply for study scholarships, which have no repayment, interest, or student debt.

What are Chevening Scholarships?

The Chevening Scholarships are awarded to individuals with leadership potential and strong academic backgrounds. These scholarships offer full financial support to the students who want to study for any eligible master’s degree at any UK university. It was started in 1983 and is funded by the Foreign, Commonwealth, Development Office and Partner organizations. The fully-funded UK study scholarships provide tuition fees, living expenses and return flights. It offers more than 1,500 study scholarships in an academic year, thereby benefitting more than fifty thousand students over the years in building their future. 

How to get a Scholarship to study in the UK?

UK study scholarships are available to those who are looking to pursue a master’s degree in the UK. You can get the scholarship to study in the UK by applying at www.chevening.org/apply, and your application will be assessed on the basis of the pre-defined eligibility criteria. When students apply for a scholarship to study in the UK, their applications undergo a selection process through a reading committee. After that, Some selected applicants are invited to submit references and attend an interview at the British High Commission. Thereafter the final selection is made. The successful applicants are announced in June so that they could commence studies in the month of September. 

What is Rhodes Scholarship in India?

Rhodes Scholarship is the oldest international scholarship and has been in place for over a century. It is available for full-time postgraduate courses offered by Oxford University. Rhodes Scholarship India covers course fees and an annual stipend to pay all living expenses of the student while studying abroad. Other than that, the Rhodes Trust covers the fee for a student visa and the international health surcharge. It also covers the economy-class flight to and from the UK. 

How do you get a Rhodes Scholarship? 

The selection of the Rhodes Scholarship is based on the outstanding intellect, character, leadership and commitment of the student. If you want to apply for the Rhodes Scholarship India? Then, you can simply go to  www.rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk/scholarships/applications to check your eligibility according to your course and institution. While applying for a Rhodes Scholarship, you must also provide a personal statement and reference letters. It's scholarship selection process is completed through a final interview. 

What is Commonwealth Scholarship in India?

The UK’s commitment to providing Commonwealth Scholarships enables around eight hundred students to take up postgraduate studies at UK universities. Other than that, Commonwealth scholarships are funded by the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office, and It covers the tuition fees, airfare to and from the UK, as well as a living allowance of the students. Almost thirty thousand students have benefitted from it over the years. You also become a member of the Alumni Network. 

Commonwealth Scholarship Eligibility

To be eligible for a Commonwealth Scholarship, you must be a citizen of a Commonwealth country and have an undergraduate honours degree in the upper second class. Also, you need a scanned copy of your passport, University degree, two references and your admission letter. If you want to know more regarding the Eligibility criteria of the Commonwealth Scholarship, you can visit www.cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/apply. The deadline for applications is in December.  

Saltire Scholarships & How to Apply for it?

Saltire scholarships are provided by the Scottish government along with Scottish universities and colleges. These scholarships are available to about fifty students where over £ 8,000 are offered for the tuition fees for one year of full-time study on a Master’s course. The living cost of the students is not covered under this plan. 

If you want to apply for the Saltire scholarship, then you must have an offer of a place at a Scottish university. The application process begins in March and you can find more details regarding the same at scotland.org

What is British Council Scholarship for Women, and How to apply for it?

British Council Scholarship for Women is given to women with a degree in science, technology, engineering or mathematics to take up higher studies at a UK university. It is a new concept which is launched by the British Council in partnership with twenty-six universities. The support includes tuition fees, stipend, travel costs, visa and health coverage. To apply for the British Council Scholarship for Women, you have to show that you need financial support to continue your studies. You should also show that you are active in the field with relevant work experience. If you want to know more regarding the same, then you can visit www.britishcouncil.org/study-work-abroad. You also need to fulfil the language requirements for the same. The deadline for its application is in April. 

GREAT Scholarships 

GREAT Scholarships worth more than ten thousand pounds and cover the tuition costs for a one-year postgraduate course in the UK. Other than that, these scholarships are jointly funded by a UK government campaign and British Council along with participating educational institutions. You can find out more regarding the same at study-uk.britishcouncil.org

What You Should Look Into  

1.The Award: How much does the UK study scholarship offer a student and how many such awards are given each year? How do you receive it and when? What costs does it cover? 

2.Eligibility: What are the various criteria that the applicant needs to fulfil? This can vary from course of study to personal circumstances. You might also be required to make certain commitments to activities that are obligatory. 

3.The Process: What are the documents or other evidence that you need to submit? Each scholarship has its own requirements such as a personal interview, references or a personal statement. 

4.Deadline: Don’t let your time and effort as well as the financial support go waste because you were not aware of the specified dates.

FAQ

How much percentage is required to get a scholarship in UK for an international student?

Though average scores are required to get admission to UK universities, but to be eligible to get a scholarship in UK a student needs to score 60-80% in their program.

Can I get full scholarship in UK for Master?
You can get scholarships for post graduate programs from UK government that will cover cost of living while you study in UK and course fee. International students who are from EU aren’t eligible for these scholarships. International students including those from Malta and Cyprus are eligible to apply for Commonwealth scholarships. 
Who is eligible to get scholarship in UK?
You must be a citizen of a commonwealth nation to be eligible for commonwealth scholarships. Moreover, you must have a upper second class honours degree to be eligible to apply for the scholarship. 
How much CGPA is needed to get a scholarship in UK?
The CGPA requirements needed to apply for scholarships in UK depend on the university and scholarship you are applying for. majority of the universities require a minimum aggregate of at least 70% for applying to a scholarship. The CGPA required is 3.5+ out of a scale of 4 or 9 out of a scale of 10. A grade A is also required consistently for a 3 to 4-years period of their degree. 
How much scholarship can an Indian student get in UK?
The amount of scholarship being offered by a particular university depends on the student profile. This amount can vary from 1000 to 6000 pounds, whereas, doctoral programs in the UK are fully funded.
